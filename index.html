Geothermal and Ground Waters Exploration

Growing concerns about climate change seen in clean alternative energy is an option to reduce the emission of polluting gases into the atmosphere. The power generation from geothermal sources is a practical alternative to being friendly to the environment because it does not affect the ecosystem by not having to inundate large areas for energy generation. Colombia has great potential for research in this area. Geofields with Magnetotelluric method provides an important tool for locating geothermal sources capable of moving with the aid of steam turbines and generate clean energy.
